Cat, Cat, Cat.

My name is Nana Takada, and this is my collection of cat-inspired artwork, painted with Procreate. Under my artist name, Sherlock Takada, I capture the charm, mystery, and playfulness of cats through digital art. In addition to my original works, I’ve also curated a selection of cat-related masterpieces from the Met, celebrating the timeless connection between art and feline muses. This portfolio is a blend of personal creativity and historical inspiration—welcome to my world of cats in art!
